ĐÁP ÁN DUO GAME - VÒNG 1:

1. Viet Nam is__multicultural country with 54 ethnic groups.

  • A. a

  • B. an

  • C. the

  • D. A and C

2. Tet is an occasion for family____in Viet Nam

  • A. visiting

  • B. meeting

  • C. reunions

  • D. seeing

3. Saint Giong was unable to talk, smile, or walk___he was three years old

  • A. If

  • B. Because

  • C. While

  • D. Even though

4. Would you like to go__a walk__the park this afternoon?

  • A. to - at

  • B. for - at

  • C. to - in

  • D. for - in

5. At school, the teacher and students___follow the rules

  • A. has to

  • B. have to

  • C. need to

  • D. haven't to

6. Children need a caring environment to develop mentally physically

  • A. and - and

  • B. both - and

  • C. the - the

  • D. in - and

7. The US is a ___country with over 80 ethnic groups

  • A. multi - cultural

  • B. bi - lingual

  • C. rich

  • D. ethnic

8. So many people nowadays are ___ computers and mobile phones. They use them a lot

  • A. addicted

  • B. bored

  • C. worried about

  • D. afraid of

9. The exhibition in the museum shows the____of different cultural groups

  • A. diversity

  • B. community

  • C. tradition

  • D. richness

10. In the Central Highlands, the biggest and tallest house in the village is the___house

  • A. communication

  • B. community

  • C. communal

  • D. commune
