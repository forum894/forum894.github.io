Top 5 Tet beautiful flowers of Vietnam

...Yori...
Vote: 11

Traditional New Year's upcoming Vietnam nation. I wish all of you a friend full of joy and much happiness. I collected 20 flowers just beautiful and very meaningful to them in the holiday home, invite the whole family to admire, choose offline!

1). Apricot blossom

Only in the spring apricot bloom bright yellow new. Common flower in the south of our country. In traditions of Vietnam, apricot symbolizes spring, good luck, happiness. Leopard is also a symbol of fidelity in love for punctuality bloom in spring time. So Leopard assigned a very important task in nature, it is timely to remind spring. Besides, giving others a branch or a flower pot mai mai is expressed desire friendship lasting cohesion.

2). Peach blossom

Peach blossom common in northern regions of our country and some cold countries. Flowers bloom in the spring of this symbol of luck, happiness, friendship clings, permanent close. Distilling a peach tree in your home or donated to someone you love is expressed wishes good life, happiness, perfection, joy and calm.

  1. Lisianthus

Flowers express a natural, comfortable, everything is the best, good health, good luck. In dictionary Chinese - Vietnamese, Cat (Kiet) means good, the wall means blessed / good or good thing.

  1. Roses

Dubbed as the lord of flowers, roses are very much loved. This flower is also respected as a messenger of love worldwide.

  1. Hydrangeas

Hydrangeas says sincerity. Hydrangeas popular in Vietnam, especially in Dalat.
