bài này mình viết tiếng anh ai đọc đc thì xuống nguồn đọc tiếng việt nha 1 Lychee "household"

The lychee is a very popular fruit, but the "green" lychee with its red and green skin is considered a "rare and hard to find" specialty from southern China, with the most expensive price. world.

Lychee "household" is not sold by weight, but only by fruit. They were also sold at auction for 550,000 yuan (more than 1.9 billion), equivalent to the price of a Mercedes car (Photo: Sohu).

2 Apple Sekai Ichi Sekai Ichi is the largest and most delicious apple in the world, originating from Japan, researched and tested by people for 46 years before being released to the market. This apple variety has a sweet and aromatic taste, selling for 21 USD/fruit (about 478,000 VND) (Photo: Taste Atlas).

3 Strawberry Queen Sembikiya

Through the extremely meticulous care process, Sembikiya strawberries are considered the queen of berries, becoming a famous specialty of the farmers of Sembikiya (Japan). They are planted, cared for and harvested after 45 days. However, each year, people here only pick about 500 Sembikiya strawberries. Because the number of strawberries produced is quite limited, this fruit is expensive, about 85 USD (more than 1.9 million VND / tray of 12 fruits) (Photo: Asian Insprirations).

4 Square watermelon

With a unique appearance that is not mixed with any fruit, square watermelon has become a famous specialty that is sought after by the rich. Each of these watermelons weighs an average of about 5kg, sold for 800 USD (more than 18 million VND). To produce this type of melon, farmers have to grow them in square boxes. Watermelon has a fancy shape, so it is often used as a gift for precious guests (Photo: India Times).

5 Pineapple Heligan

Pineapple is a familiar fruit in the tropics, but the pineapples grown in the Heligan garden in the UK are considered a "rare and hard to find" specialty. They undergo special care with strict temperature standards. It takes up to 2 years for farmers to harvest Heligan pineapples. This pineapple is large and heavy, can reach 13kg / fruit. Their price is also extremely expensive, about 1,500 USD / fruit (more than 23.7 million VND) (Photo: India Times).

6 Mango Taiyo no Tomago

Taiyo no Tomago mango (also known as Sun egg) is a fruit grown manually in the Miyazaki region, Japan. This mango variety is difficult to grow and is wrapped in special mesh bags during growth to ensure that they always receive the right amount of sunlight. This fruit has a strong aroma and sweet taste.
