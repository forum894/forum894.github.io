<pre> `đố ai dịch đc chữ ở cap ^^` </pre>

1. Câu đồng tình khẳng định

  • [S + V , too = So + V + S]

EX: I like beef and my brother does, too / so does my brother.

She is a teacher and I am, too / so am I.

We can draw and they can, too / so can they.

2 . Câu đồng tình phủ định

  • [S + V + not + either = Neither + V + S ]

EX: He isn't a doctor and she isn't either / Neither is she.

I didn't do my homework and my friend didn't either / Neither did my friend.

We aren't hungry and they aren't either / Neither are they.

LƯU Ý : Nếu dùng Neither thì chia khẳng định nhaa :>
