  5. (the dog/ not/ play with a ball)

  6. (why/ it/ rain now?)

  7. (how/ she/ travel?)

  8. (where/ you/ work?)

  9. (Sarah/ not/ tell a story)

  10. (Sarah/ not/ tell a story)

II Cho dạng đúng của động từ trong ngoặc:

  1. Look! The car (go) so fast. ( đã có người làm )

  2. Listen! Someone (cry) in the next room. ( đã có người làm )

  3. Your brother (sit) next to the beautiful girl over there at present? ( đã có người làm )

  4. Now they (try) to pass the examination.( đã có người làm )

  5. It’s 12 o’clock, and my parents (cook) lunch in the kitchen. ( đã có người làm )

  6. Keep silent! You (talk) so loudly. ( đã có người làm )

  7. I (not stay) at home at the moment. ( đã có người làm )

  8. Now she (lie) to her mother about her bad marks. ( đã có người làm )

  9. At present they (travel) to New York. ( đã có người làm )

  10. He (not work) in his office now. ( đã có người làm )

III Đặt các động từ vào mẫu chính xác

  1. It (get)....... dark. Shall I turn on the light?

  2. They don't have any where to live at the moment. They(stay)...... with friends until they find somewhere.

  3. Why are all these people here? What (happen)..........?

  4. Where is your mother? She........ (have) dinner in the kitchen

  5. The student (not, be)..... in class at present

  6. Some people (not drink)............ coffee now.

  7. At present, he (compose)............ a piece of music

  8. We (have)............. dinner in a resataurant right now
