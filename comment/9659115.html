  • lá 1 : tên : MEKTIMED BLAST

-NỘI DUNG : Target 1 " Meklord " monster you control and 1 card your opponent controls , destroy them .

  • lá 2 : tên : TGXT -HL

  • NỘI DUNG : Target 1 " T . G " monster you control ; its ATK and DEF each become half its current ATK and DEF , and if they do , destroy 1 other Spell / Trap on the field

-lá 3 : tên : FLAMING DANCE - DUBHE

  • NỘI DUNG : As long as this card exists on the field , in addition to a summons , you can use usually summon for on Beast fighters monster only once in your main phase . Moreover , as long as this card exists on the field , the ATK of the Beast fighters monster is raised 100 point on your field.
