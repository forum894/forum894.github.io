Characteristics

The cause of this transparent wing phenomenon is due to the surface structure of the Glasswing butterfly. The wings of this butterfly have a random nanostructure, arranged in a non-specific order. So when sunlight hits, most of the light rays will pass through the above structure, leading to the invisible butterfly phenomenon. Glasswing butterflies possess a completely chaotic wing structure, to the point of achieving an invisible effect that makes a huge evolutionary step in helping Glasswing butterflies survive in the wild because the more colorful the butterfly, the easier it is for them to be seen by predators. and chase.
