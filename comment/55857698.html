Sauu 1 tuần mày mò khắp nơi thì mik cx đã thu thập đủ 16 cụm từ vt tắt trong tiếng Anh (mettmoii)أ‿أ. Okay và cùng mik tìm hỉu típ 1 số cụm từ vt tắt mak "Tây" hay dùng trong chat hoặc voice nkaaa (ʃƪ˘･ᴗ･˘)

Usta= used to (đã từng lmj)

  • I used to work here. => I usta work here. =======================================================================

Supposta= supposed to (phải lmj)

  • What am I supposed to do. => What am I supposta do. =======================================================================

A lotta= a lot of (nhiều)

  • There're a lot of people. => There're a lotta people. =======================================================================

Lotsa= lost of (nhiều) -> Omm cái nèi mik tự suy ra cụa từ bên trên đó:> Nên là cx ko chắc vt đúm khôm >:?

  • She has lost of money. => She has lotsa money. =======================================================================

Needa= need to (cần phải lmj)

  • You don't need to do that. => You don't needa do that. =======================================================================

Oughta= ought to (nên lmj)

  • You ought to talk to her. => You oughta talk to her. =======================================================================

Hafta= have to (phải lmj)

  • I have to do the dishes. => I hafta do the dishes. =======================================================================

Hasta= has to (phải lmj)

  • He has to leave early. => He hasta leave early. =======================================================================

Musta= must have (chắc là đã lmj)

  • He must have been late. => He musta been late. =======================================================================

Coulda= could have (có thể đã lmj)

  • I could have helped you. => I coulda helped you. =======================================================================

Woulda= would have (sẽ lmj)

  • I would have done the same. => I woulda done the same. =======================================================================

Shoulda= should have (đáng lẽ nên lmj)

  • You should have joined us. => You shoulda joined us. =======================================================================

Mighta= might have (có khả năng đã lmj)

  • I might have seen them. => I mighta seen them. =======================================================================

D'you?= do you? (bn có...ko?)

  • Do you want to hang out? => D'you want to hang out? =======================================================================

Innit?= isn't it? (phải ko?)

  • It's hot, isn't it ? => It's hot, innit ? =======================================================================

Whatcha= what are you (bn đag...?)

  • What are you doing here? => Whatcha doing here?
