The first, the pronoun I is always capitalized, no matter it starts the sentence or not. You can get a reference for this here: "http://grammar.yourdictionary.com/capitalization/10-rules-of-capitalization.html". That error seems basic but it's so important.

And other errors such as "when" in "I am very happy when see u", "live" in "live in the...", "year old", "I work in the a big hospital", "the a small house", "I love my...so I am very happy", "like to"
