3/Bài văn tả về Tết

Hello everyone, I'm from Vietnam. And now I will tell you about the very famous festival in Vietnam, Tet. Before Tet, people buy the new clothes, fruits and flowers. In the North of Vietnam, people buy peach trees and in the south, people buy apricot trees. Everyone cleans their houses. On New Year's Eve, everyone wakes up waiting for the New Year. Fireworks start right after the arrival of a New Year. During Tet, Tet music resounds in everyone's joy. People visit their family and friends. Children wear new clothes and get lucky money. Everyone is happy and excited about the New Year. I love it very much! And you? Do you like Tet?
