The boastful turtle

A selfish turtle wanted to fly, and he would have done anything to make it happen. Turtle was angry that his flippers weren't wings and that his shell made him too heavy to fly. He was angry that other turtles were happy with swimming, and acted bitterfly toward birds who could do the thing he could only dream of. Turtle finally asked, or rather demanded, that a goose help him fly. The geese came up with an idea to hold a stick that Turtle could hold onto with his strong mouth. While he was flying, selfish Turtle could not help but open his mouth to boast to all of the turtles below who would never fly like him, but he forgot that letting go would make him fall out of the sky.

Don't be boastful or bossy to others and don't feel the need to brag about your abilities.

Boastful (adj.): thích khoe khoang

Selfish (adj.): ích kỷ

Flipper (n.): chân chèo

Shell (n.): vỏ, mai

Act bitterly (v.): đối xử hằn học

Demand (v.): yêu cầu, đòi hỏi

Goose (n.): con ngỗng

Bossy (adj.): hợm hĩnh

Brag (v.): khoe khoang khoác lác

Cái này không có nguồn. Mình mua sách về rồi đăng lên.
