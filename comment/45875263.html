Chữa bài

Huy couldn't go to school because he is ill.

→ Because of...

...his illness, Huy couldn't go to school.

Would you like to go to the zoo with me?

→ How about...

...going to the zoo with me?

When did you buy that coat?

→ How long is...

...it since you bought that coat?

They spent a whole day looking for these old toys.

→ It took...

...them a whole day to look for these old toys.

My sister doesn't like candies either.

→ Neither...

...does my sister like candifier.
