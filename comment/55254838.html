Kiến thức của lớp 7 nek. Hơi ít chút mong mn thông cảm. Ngoài lớp 7, mn cx có thể zô tham khảo nha

  1. Compound sentences

  2. Clase ( mệnh đề ) : S + V

  3. Compound sentences : 2 clause

Ex : She is happy but I am sad

<pre> S1 S2 and S2 V2 or so </pre>

=> She is happy but I am sad

  1. Form : S + started / began + doing sth + time +ago<pre> S + have/has + Vpp + fort time </pre>

Ex : I started learning English for 4 year

=> I have learning English for 4 year

  1. Form : When did + S + V ?

= How long + have/has + V + Vpp ?

Ex : How long have you learning English ?

  1. Form : This is the first time + S + have/has + Vpp

= S + have/has + never '+ Vpp + before

Ex : This is the first time I have been to Japan

= I have never been to Japan before
