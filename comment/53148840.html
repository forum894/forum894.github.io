A morning of a calico cat

In the early morning, when the rooster from the huge wooden carving wall clock pops out and crows a squealing noise, I have to sunbathe. It is not challenging for me to thread through the window fence, jump out to the yard, scratch and scratch the plumeria trunk, hop nimbly on the branch and over the wall. The house is surrounded with red-brick high wall which stars to be covered in moss after years. I fit the wall , and doze under the morning light. Some random vehicles pass by, such noise quickly fades into the quiet atmosphere...
