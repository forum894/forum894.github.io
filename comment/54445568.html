Dùng để diễn tả một hành động đang xảy ra trong quá khứ tại một thời điểm xác định.

  1. Khẳng định

We/They/ You+ were+ V-ing

I/ He/ She/ It+ was+ V-ing

VD: He was going to the parl at 7a.m yesterday

  1. Phủ định

We/They/ You+ weren't+ V-ing

I/He/ She/ It+ wasn't+ V-ing

VD: We weren't going to the cinema at 8p.m yesterday

  1. Nghi vấn

Was+ I/ He/ She/ It+ V-ing.....?

Were+ you/ we/ they+ V-ing.....?

VD: Was she playing the piano this time last week?

Diễn tả hai hành động xảy ra đồng thời trong quá khứ

while I was playing football, my sister was cooking
