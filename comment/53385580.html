(# Learning Grammar)☆ Use HOW MUCH/MANY; A FEW; A LITTLE, LOT OF.

1/ How much. - use with : Uncountable nouns. *EX: How much money have you got? - How much food we have for this party?

2/ How many. - use with plurals countables nouns *EX: -How many students are there in your class ? - How many people are coming to our party?

3/ A little. - = not much. - use with: uncountables noun * EX: Can I borrow a little sugar, please ? - There is still a little time left.

4/ A few - = not many - use with plural countables nouns * EX: Can I borrow a few cups, please? - There are a few good films on TV tonight.

5/ A lot of - = a large number - use with plural countable nouns and uncountable nouns * EX: There are a lot of people in the market today. - We have got a lot of money in the bank.

6/ No - = not any - plural countable nouns and uncountable nouns. * EX: There are no vegetables in the kitchen. - There is no milk in the fridge.
