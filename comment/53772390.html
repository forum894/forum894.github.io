  • Rendang - Indonesian dry curry

    Rendang is a famous dish in Indonesia, originating from Minangkabau (also known as Padang) in West Sumatra. Usually, Rendang is made from beef stewed in coconut milk along with delicious spices until the water dries up, the meat is soft and fully absorbed. The main spices used to create the unique spicy remoah flavor for this dish are lemongrass, galangal, garlic, turmeric, ginger and chili peppers. This is one of the traditional Indonesian dishes. But if you want to change the taste, you can also "variate" it from liver, chicken, goat or other meats… Because of the rich cooking ingredients and the perfect combination of spices, Rendang also considered the most delicious and nutritious food in the world.

Neta and sushi presentation are varied, but the main ingredient that all sushi has is shari. The most popular neta is seafood. Although sushi is only a small serving size, only 1/3 of the serving size of Asian or European dishes, it is still loved by diners because of its rich and varied ingredients and unique creative presentation. , spectacular.

  • Tom Yum - Thailand

This is a typical Thai hot and sour soup. The main ingredients to cook tom Yum are shrimp or chicken and mushrooms. Indispensable spices in the soup include Thai lime leaves, lemongrass root, young galangal root, dried onions, fresh chili (or satay, dried chili), lime juice or tamarind juice, herbs (coriander, coriander) , basil), tomatoes (can be replaced with cashew oil for color), coconut water or coconut milk, broth with chicken or pork bones, Thai fish sauce, sugar, salt, seasoning. Due to the variety and picky about how to choose ingredients and spices, Yum shrimp has its own unique flavor of the land of the golden pagoda and is loved by diners around the world. Tom Yum ranked 8th in the top 50 most delicious and popular dishes in the world voted by CNN.

Since its appearance, Peking duck is not only a favorite dish of Chinese people but also of many people around the world. Anyone who has ever tasted it will not be able to forget the charming taste of this dish.

The second Japanese dish on this list is the famous Ramen. A special feature is that diners will encounter thousands of noodle shops all over Japan, but the taste is not the same.   • Lasagna - Italy

Lasagna is a flat, very wide noodle (sometimes with wavy edges). It is usually prepared as a layer on top of each other with cheese, sauces, and often other ingredients such as meat or vegetables. Lasagna can be considered the pinnacle of pasta art. This dish is made from flat pasta, stacked alternately with cheese, beef or vegetables.

  • Bibimbap - Korea

Korean food culture is known for its colorful and eye-catching dishes. Bibimbap is one of the typical dishes symbolizing the culinary culture of the land of kimchi.

Usually, a bowl of Bibimbap will have from 6 to 7 dishes. The white of the rice, the yellow of the egg, the green of the vegetables, the brown of the meat, all mixed well with the sauce made from chili. The perfect blend of the flavors of each dish with the tongue-slicing spiciness of chili peppers will stimulate any discerning diners' taste buds. Not only beautiful and delicious, but bibimbap is also extremely nutritious.

  • Thai hot pot - delicious food forget the way home

Diverse and attractive culinary culture is one of the factors attracting tourists to the land of golden pagodas. Thai hot pot - a delicious dish that anyone who comes to Thailand wants to enjoy.

The ingredients for making this hot pot dish are extremely diverse, mainly including: fresh seafood (shrimp, squid, oysters, ...), mushrooms, beef and indispensable green vegetables. The characteristic sour taste of hot pot sauce mixed with the spicy taste of chili, the sweetness of bone broth, the aroma of onions and lemongrass will make you want to eat forever.

  • Singapore frog porridge

  • Curry - an indispensable dish of the Indian people

Referring to India without mentioning curry, is really a big omission. Curry appears in all Indian meals. Like Vietnamese pho or Japanese sushi, curry is considered the national soul of Indian cuisine.

If you are visiting the beautiful city of Rome, don't forget to stop by any of the restaurants here to enjoy delicious Pasta.

  • Macaron cake - quintessence of French cuisine

This is a very popular cake in France. The cake is made from egg whites, powdered sugar, granulated sugar, almond flour and a little food coloring. Macaron filling is filled with jam, ganache and butter.

Feel the soft, sweet taste of sugar, butter eggs and the chewy, sour taste of jam. There's nothing better than sipping some tea. Macaron cake is not only famous in France and in European countries, it is also easy to see this cake in coffee shops, pastry shops or parties.

  • Coxinhas - Typical Brazilian street food

Exciting, hot like samba dances. Brazilian cuisine has a very own and unique feature. The beautiful little Coxinhas cake is one of the typical street foods here.

  • Apple pie - traditional food in America

Apple pie is considered a symbol of American culture and prosperity. This is an indispensable dessert after every meal in American families, especially in North America.   • Onion soup - France

If you love European cuisine and want to change the taste of familiar European dishes, don't hesitate to try the French onion soup recipe for an attractive and enchanting appetizer below.

French onion soup has its origins in Roman times and began to gain popularity as a unique appetizer in the 18th century.   • Wiener Schnitzel - Austria

Wiener Schnitzel is a typical fried veal dish in Austrian culinary culture. This dish is not only popular in Austria but also spread to many other European countries such as Switzerland, Germany, etc. The name “Wiener Schnitzel” first appeared in the late 19th century.
