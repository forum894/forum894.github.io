VỊ TRÍ

Before : Đằng trước

Ex : He is standing before me.

Behind: Đằng sau

Ex : My house is behind the school.

Under-Below: dưới, ở phía dưới

Ex : My pen is under a book.

Ex :Cat is lying below the table.

Above-Over : trên, ở phía trên

Ex : There is a halo over/above my head.

By-Next to-Beside : Kế bên

Ex : The school is next to/by/beside the hospital.

Near : Gần

Ex : The Coffee House is near my house.

Between: giữa

I sit between my father and my mother.
